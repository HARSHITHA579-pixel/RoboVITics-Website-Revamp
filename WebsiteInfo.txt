SECTION 1 — HOME

================================================================================

Taglines / Mottos:

  - "A place to learn, a chance to grow"

  - "Innovation is when Imagination meets Ambition"


RoboVITics is the official Robotics Club of VIT, Vellore. It is a group of

ardent tech enthusiasts with the zeal to learn, to build, and a thirst to be

the best. The club organizes free workshops, seminars, and competitions, and

its members collaborate on projects that represent VIT at national and

international levels.


================================================================================

SECTION 2 — ABOUT VIT

================================================================================

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a private deemed university founded

by G. Viswanathan in 1984. Its mission is to improve students' lives through

excellence in education and research.


The Department of Student Welfare plays an irreplaceable role in creating a

lively, fun, and resourceful community for students. Every year they join

hands with VIT's Clubs and Chapters so that every student finds an environment

where they can learn and grow together.

SECTION 3 — ABOUT US

================================================================================

RoboVITics is a group of ardent tech enthusiasts with the zeal to learn, to

build, and a thirst to be the best. What started as a small group of passionate

techies exploring different domains of technology has blossomed into the

official Robotics Club of VIT, Vellore.


MISSION:

  To spread knowledge about robotics by organizing various free workshops and

  seminars that help people find their technical niches and build their own

  projects.


WHAT THEY DO:

  - Collaborate on incredible projects

  - Nurture glorious teams that represent VIT all over the world

  - Hold several accolades from national and international competitions

  - Uphold the legacy left by predecessors (all members called "RoboVITians")

SECTION 4 — EVENTS

================================================================================


4.1  EQUINOX

     A jam-packed 36-hour hackathon/tech session where participants can share

     ideas and creativity, valued by an enthusiastic audience. Mentors and mini

     workshops with abundant resources are available throughout the event.


4.2  VORTEX 360

     A CAD modelling hackathon powered by AutoDesk. Designers are inspired to

     let their creative juices flow and elevate their designs into something

     truly original and useful.

     Theme: "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like.

             Design is how it works."


4.3  MACHINE DESIGN

     A workshop using Fusion 360 to turn vivid design imaginations into modelled

     reality. Participants interact with a great community of Fusion 360

     designers and experts, exploring CAD and modern design trends.


4.4  HANDS ON ROBOTICS

     RoboVITics' annual workshop focused on learning basic skills to design fully

     functional mobile Arduino robots. Bots built include:

       - Line Follower

       - Obstacle Avoider

       - Other computer-controlled bots using various sensors


4.5  ROBOWARS

     The flagship event of Gravitas (VIT's annual tech fest). A Robot Combat

     Competition where participants from around the globe battle for the coveted

     RoboWars Champion Title. "Sparks fly as metal crushes metal."

SECTION 8 — PROJECTS

================================================================================

Members collaborate across all domains to build innovative robotics and

technology projects.


Notable Highlighted Project:

  SPIDERBOT

  A robot with a spider-like design and a unique walking mechanism based on

  the Klann linkage mechanism, which helps it move through rough terrain with

  ease. The 8-legged bot is assembled using 3D-printed components and can be

  programmed to perform dangerous tasks such as:

    - Bomb defusing

    - Land mine detection

    - Underwater surveillance

  (Used in scenarios where human involvement is impossible or very risky.)
